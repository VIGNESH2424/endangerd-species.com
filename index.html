Endangerd species

Thirteen bird species have gone extinct in the Americas in the past 50 years, most in Hawaii: Kauai Akialoa, Guam Reed-warbler, Colombian Grebe, Least Vermilion Flycatcher, Bishop's Oo, Guam Flycatcher, Bridled White-eye, Kamao, Atitlan Grebe, Kauai Oo, Po'ouli, Cryptic Treehunter, and Alagoas Foliage-gleaner.
